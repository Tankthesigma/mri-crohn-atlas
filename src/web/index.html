Technical Approach

Methodology

1
Literature Search
Systematic search of PubMed and Google Scholar for perianal Crohn's disease MRI studies reporting VAI or MAGNIFI-CD scores (2003-2025).
2
Data Extraction
LLM-assisted extraction of aggregate VAI/MAGNIFI scores from 17 studies representing 2,818 patients across multiple treatment modalities.
3
Fibrosis Inference
Novel latent variable scoring (0-6) inferred from clinical descriptions of healing, treatment response, and tissue characteristics.
4
Neuro-Symbolic Regression
OLS regression with indicator function for healed cases. The I(VAIâ‰¤2) term applies fibrosis adjustment only when VAI â‰¤ 2.
5
Validation
10-fold cross-validation, leave-one-study-out, and 1000-iteration bootstrap for confidence intervals.
6
Threshold Alignment
Verification that predicted MAGNIFI ranges align with established clinical severity categories.
1
LLM Selection
DeepSeek-Chat via OpenRouter API. Selected for strong medical text comprehension and cost efficiency.
2
Prompt Engineering
Multi-stage extraction with conservative scoring rules. Explicit instructions to avoid overscoring.
3
Post-Processing
Rule-based fallbacks for seton detection, abscess handling, and treatment-aware T2 adjustment.
4
Confidence Calibration
Empirically-tuned formula blending rule-based (70%) and LLM (30%) confidence with conservative cap at 85%.
5
Score Calculation
Deterministic VAI and MAGNIFI scoring from extracted features using published component weights.
